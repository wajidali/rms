About

Small businesses are often remarkably talented at focusing on what they do best. However, that often leaves little time to search for information and contacts that can help them grow. Trade is Key gives these businesses an easy to use single point of access to that support.

Contact Us

Trade barriers affect small companies far more than those with the staff and resources to overcome them. Trade is Key gives firms the ability to voice the challenges they face in their region and industry.

From dual certification demands to tariff barriers there are many policy decisions that need input from stakeholders. Trade is Key provides small businesses with a virtual advocacy tool where decision makers can easily find and address these practical obstacles to growth.
